= <Forest-amid Moon Night> (<林中月夜>) (CE 1992-08) (by: 金 波) =

	溶溶的月光, 像银亮的春水: 洒在每一片绿叶上.
+	Molten moonlight, like silver-bright Spring water: Sprinkled upon each piece green leaf.
	闪着耀眼的光辉.
+	Glimmering dazzling glowing.

	叶子一动不动: 做着恬静的梦.
+	Leaves unmove: making the tranquil dream.
	梦见月光化作露珠: 一样的晶莹, 一样的玲珑:
+	Dreamt moonlight transformed into dews: same lucid, same translucent:
	.
	在晨风里摇落:
+	In the dawn breeze shake-off:
	一滴滴: 叮咚, 叮, 咚.
+	Drop by drop: tington, ting, ton.

----
