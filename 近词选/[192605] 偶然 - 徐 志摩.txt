= <Coincidence> (<偶然>) (CE 1926-05) (by: 徐 志摩) =

	我, 是天空里的一片云.
+	I, am a cloud in the sky.
	偶尔, 投影在你的波心.
+	Occasionally, overlayed at your wave center.
	你不必讶异, 更无须欢喜:
+	You do not have to amaze, nor really delight:
	在转瞬间, 消灭了踪影.
+	At transience, dispersed the trace.

	你我相逢在黑夜的海上.
+	You and I met over the nightly sea.
	你有你的, 我有我的: 方向.
+	You have yours, I have mine: direction.
	你记得也好, 最好你忘掉:
+	Well you remember, better you forget:
	在这交会时: 互放的光亮.
+	During the moment of intersection: the emitting light.
