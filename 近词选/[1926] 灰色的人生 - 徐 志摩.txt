= <The Grayish Life> (<灰色的人生>) (~ CE 1926) (by: 徐 志摩) =

	来! 我邀你们到民间去.
+	Come! I invite you all to the worldly.
	听: 衰老的, 病痛的, 贫苦的, 残毁的, 受压迫的, 烦闷的, 懦怯的, 丑陋的, 罪恶的:
+	Hear: decayed, ill, impoverished, ruined, depressed, stifling, cowardish, ugly, sinful:
	和着深秋的风声与雨声, 合唱的: <灰色的人生>.
+	Accompanied with deep Autumn's wind and rain, symphonied: <The Grayish Life>.
