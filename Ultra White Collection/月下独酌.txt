= <Moon Below Alone Drinking> (<月下独酌>) (~ CE 744-04) (by: 李 白) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=月下独酌&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=李白

----

	花间一壶酒: 独酌无相亲.
+	Flowers amid 1 flagon wine: Alone drinking without affinitive.
	举杯邀明月: 对影成三人.
+	Rise cup inviting bright moon: Reflected shadow be 3 persons.

	月既不解饮: 影徒随我身.
+	Moon since couldn't resolve drinking: Shadow vainly accompanying my self.
	暂伴月将影: 行乐须及春.
+	Periodic accompany Moon about Shadow: Make merry shall at Spring.
	.
	我歌月徘徊: 我舞影零乱.
+	I sing Moon wanders by: I dance Shadow much scattered.
	醒时相交欢: 醉后各分散.
+	Sober times each acquainting joyous: Drunk after each there depart.

	永结无情游: 相期邈云汉.
+	Forever binding the tour emotionless: Each expecting immense cloudy abroad.
