= <Juniors Tour> (<少年行>) (CE 731) (by: 李 白) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=少年行&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=李白#poem_25563

----

	五陵年少金市东: 银鞍白马度春风.
+	5 Mounds juniors Gold Fair east: Silver saddle white horse spent Spring vibe.
	落花踏尽游何处? 笑入胡姬酒肆中.
+	Fallen flowers stepped off tour be where? Laughing entered wine house of foreign ladies.
