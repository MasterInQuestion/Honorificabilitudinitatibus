= <Night Lodged Mountain Temple> (<夜宿山寺>) (by: (~ CE 730) 李 白; (~ CE 982) 杨 亿) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=恐惊天上人&ClauseIndex=content

----

	危楼高百尺:
+	Lingering belvedere hundreds Chǐ high:
	手可摘星辰.
+	Hand may reach the stars.
	不敢高声语:
+	Dare not talk too loud:
	恐惊天上人.
+	Lest bothering Celestials High-Up.
