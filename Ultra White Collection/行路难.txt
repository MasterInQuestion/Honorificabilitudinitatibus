= <Tour of Difficulty> (<行路难>) (CE 731) (by: 李 白) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=行路难&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=李白

----

	金樽清酒斗十千: 玉盘珍馐值万钱.
+	Golden Cup clear wine vessel tens thousand: Jade dishes rare delicacies worthy myriad credits.
	停杯投箸不能食: 拔剑四顾心茫然.
+	Cup stop dropped sticks cannot eat: Unsheathe sword look-around heart in wonder.
	.
	欲渡 黄河 冰塞川: 将登 太行 雪满山.
+	Intend crossing Yellow River ice jammed pass: About ascending Great-Line snow whole mountain.
	闲来垂钓碧溪上: 忽复乘舟梦日边.
+	At ease fishing emerald stream atop: Sudden again taking boat dreamy Sun aside.

	行路难, 行路难: 多歧路, 今安在?
+	Tour of difficulty, tour of difficulty: mostly astray, now be where?
	长风破浪会有时: 直挂云帆济沧海.
+	Long wind breaking surge shall of time: Straight hanging cloudy sails across Vast Ocean.
