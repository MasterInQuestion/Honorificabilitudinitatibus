= <Old Bright Moon Tour> (<古朗月行>) (CE 753) (by: 李 白) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=朗月行&ClauseIndex=title#poem_25989

----

	小时不识月: 呼作白玉盘.
+	Juvenescence reckoned not the moon: Calling which white jade plate.
	又疑瑶台镜: 飞在青云端.
+	Also wondered fantasy tower mirror: Flying at azure clouds atop.
