= <Playful Gifting Dù Fǔ> (<戏赠 杜 甫>) (CE 753) (by: 李 白) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=杜甫&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=李白

----

	饭颗山头逢杜甫:
+	Dining grain mountain top met Dù Fǔ (杜 甫):
	顶戴笠子日卓午.
+	Top wearing cloak there sun high-noon.
	借问别来太瘦生:
+	Pardon question since left too thin person:
	总为从前作诗苦?
+	Always for past-passé making poems suffer?
