= <Title Untitled> (<题无题>) (CE 2024-08) (by: Master ? ; (CE 879 ~ CE 883-05) 曹 松, 韦 庄; (CE 1545-02-22) 文 徵明; (CE 759) 杜 甫) =

	一将功成万骨枯: 大明宫内血成河.
+	One General's accomplishment myriads bones shrunk: Palace Great Brilliancy inside blood be river.
	甲第朱门无一半: 天街踏尽公卿骨.
+	Prime clans Red gates without one-half: Heavenly Street stomped out Lords Chancellors' bones.
	厉志开元济黎庶: 峻刑戮力定天宝.
+	Harsh will Era Established serving general public: Tough justice killing strive cast Heavenly Treasure.
	江山万古皆陈迹: 一笑无须论是非.
+	Estate all years all be relic: One smile need not discussing right wrong.

	本生瘴疠中: 何畏流毒谤?
+	Origin born hazy venomous amid: How afraid floating venoms' libel?
	功名皆速朽: 功业自人心.
+	Recognition all shall soon decay: Accomplishment since of people's heart.


	名正言且顺: 无名应不成.
+	Name proper words then smooth: No name should no accomplishment.
	无名岂无实?
+	No name truly no truth?
	.
	千秋万岁名: 寂寞身后事.
+	Thousands Autumns myriads years' fame: Lonely sole after life affairs.
