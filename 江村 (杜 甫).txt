= <River Village> (<江村>) (CE 760) (by: 杜 甫) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=江村&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=杜甫#poem_30717

----

	清江一曲抱村流: 长夏江村事事幽.
+	Clear river one song hugging village flows: Long Summer river village things affairs serene.
	自去自来梁上燕: 相亲相近水中鸥.
+	Self go self come roof-top swallows: In affinity in intimacy in-water gulls.
