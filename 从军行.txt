= <With Army Tour> (<从军行>) (CE 725) (by: 王 昌龄) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Key=从军行&ClauseIndex=title&Dynasty=唐朝&Author=王昌龄#poem_8553

----

	青海长云暗雪山:
+	Teal sea long clouds dim snowy mountain:
	孤城遥望玉门关.
+	Lonely city far view Jade Gate Pass.
	黄沙百战穿金甲:
+	Yellow sand hundreds battles pierced Gold Armor:
	不破楼兰终不还.
+	Smash not Loulan shall not return.
