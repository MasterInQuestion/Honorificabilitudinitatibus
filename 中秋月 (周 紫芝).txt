= <Mid-Autumn Moon> (<中秋月>) (~ CE 1130) (by: 周 紫芝; (CE 2024-09-15) Master ?) =

	秋云微淡月无光.
+	Autumn clouds slightly faint Moon without aura.
	云黯黯: 月彩偃.
+	Cloudy dim dim: Moon color contained.
	应是 嫦娥 心里: 也似人愁.
+	Shall be Lady Eternity in heart: also mourn as people.

	相逢不似长相忆: 一度相逢一度愁.
+	Each acquaintance unlike long each longing: Once each acquaintance once of mourn.
	一点残红欲尽时: 叶叶声声是别离.
+	One dot lingering red about to fade: Leaf-leaf sound-sound be reluctant departure.
