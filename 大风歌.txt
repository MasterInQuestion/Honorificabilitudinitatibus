= <Whirlwind Ballad> (<大风歌>) (BCE 196-11) (by: 刘 邦) =

	大风起兮云飞扬:
+	Whirlwind blows clouds ascend:
	威加海内兮归故乡.
+	Stabilized the whole Estate backing to hometown.
	安得猛士兮守四方?
+	Where may find honorable capable guarding 4 Directions?

----

	飞鸟尽: 良弓藏.
+	Flying birds exhausted: Fine bows ensheathe.
	狡兔死: 走狗烹.
+	Cunny rabbits dead: Walking dogs cooked.
