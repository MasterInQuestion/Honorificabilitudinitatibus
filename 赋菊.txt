= <Of Chrysanthemum> (<赋菊>) (~ CE 875) (by: 黄 巢) =

	https://cnkgraph.com/Writing/Search?Dynasty=唐朝&Author=黄巢#poem_47322

----

	待到秋来九月八:
+	Wait until Autumn comes Month 9, 8th:
	我花开后百花杀.
+	My flowers blossom there all flowers wither.
	冲天香阵透长安:
+	Heaven-reach Fragrance set pierced Cháng-Ān:
	满城尽带黄金甲.
+	Whole city through decorated Yellowish Golden Armor.
